# What is Edventurefy?  
  
If school is meant to prepare people for life, learning should feel more like practice for the adventure of living.  
  
Edventurefy is a methodology built around the conditions that significantly increase the chances of learning and growth:  
  
- agency  
- relevance  
- challenge  
- ownership  
- social connection  
- visible progress  
- clear next steps  
- feedback  
  
The goal is simple: upgrade learning from passive content exposure into active skill acquisition.  
  
- less compliance  
- more engagement  
- less passive coverage  
- more lived learning  
- less “test and forget”  
- more skill acquisition  
  
Edventurefy is not limited to one subject, one age group, or one delivery mode.  
  
It can be applied to:  
  
- real-life skills  
- STEM  
- group learning  
- facilitator-led adventures  
- low-tech and high-tech settings  
- online and offline environments  
  
Current public examples include:  
  
- Life’s Six Buckets  
- Drake Equation  
- Fermi Paradox  
- SETI-Edventures  
  
At its core, Edventurefy is about designing learning in a way that better matches how humans naturally learn:  
through curiosity, connection, challenge, participation, and meaning.  
  
This is not just a theory piece.  
It is a practical framework intended to be tested, adapted, improved, and used.
